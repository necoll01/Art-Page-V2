Charcoals and Pastels

Charcoals and pastels are two different mediums, but have some things in common, so I decided that they should be grouped together. They are both considered dry mediums, which usually come in the format of a stick. This makes them very portable, but this also comes with a small downfall: they can get messy!

Pastels are similar to crayons in the sense that they are both traditionally pretty waxy. Pastels are a lot easier to blend though. A lot of artist (including myself) just use a finger to blend the colors together.
